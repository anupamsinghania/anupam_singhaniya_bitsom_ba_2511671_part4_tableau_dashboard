
# Chart Selection Justification

---

# 1. Sales Trend

Business Question

How are sales changing over time?

Selected Chart

Line Chart

Reason for Selection

Sales is a time-based measure. A line chart clearly illustrates changes over time and allows executives to identify trends, seasonal fluctuations, and unusual changes.

Business Value

Supports forecasting and performance monitoring.

---

# 2. Regional Performance

Business Question

Which region generates the highest sales?

Selected Chart

Horizontal Bar Chart

Reason for Selection

Horizontal bar charts provide clear comparison between regions while accommodating longer category labels.

Business Value

Helps management compare regional performance and allocate resources effectively.

---

# 3. Category Profitability

Business Question

Which product categories generate the highest profit?

Selected Chart

Horizontal Bar Chart

Reason for Selection

Bar charts provide accurate comparison between categories and clearly display ranking.

Business Value

Supports inventory planning and product investment decisions.

---

# 4. Customer Segment

Business Question

Which customer segment contributes most to sales?

Selected Chart

Packed Bubble Chart

Reason for Selection

Bubble charts provide an intuitive visual comparison of customer segment contribution.

Business Value

Supports customer segmentation and marketing strategy.



---

# 5. Shipping Performance

Business Question

Which shipping method performs better?

Selected Chart

Horizontal Bar Chart

Reason for Selection

The chart enables quick comparison of average delivery days across shipping methods.

Business Value

Supports logistics improvement initiatives.

---

# 6. Discount vs Profit

Business Question

How do discounts influence profitability?

Selected Chart

Scatter Plot

Reason for Selection

Scatter plots effectively display relationships between two numerical variables and highlight trends or outliers.

Business Value

Helps optimize pricing and promotional strategies.

---

# 7. Return Analysis

Business Question

Which product categories experience higher return rates?

Selected Chart

Horizontal Bar Chart

Reason for Selection

Ranking categories using bars makes return comparison straightforward and easy to interpret.

Business Value

Supports quality improvement and operational cost reduction.

---

# Dashboard Design Principles

The dashboard follows standard Business Intelligence design principles:

• Executive-friendly layout

• Minimal visual clutter

• Consistent formatting

• Interactive filtering

• Appropriate chart selection

• Clear KPI presentation

• Easy comparison between business dimensions

---

# Conclusion

Every visualization was selected based on the business question it answers rather than visual appearance. This ensures the dashboard communicates information clearly, supports executive decision-making, and follows business analytics best practices.
